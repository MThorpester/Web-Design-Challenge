Project Overview

This project analyzed COVID-19 case counts and levels of anxiety and depression in the US, to better understand the impact of the pandemic on the mental health of Americans. We collected, cleaned, analyzed and visualized several CDC datasets and made some interesting discoveries that you can learn more about by exploring the plots and data on this website.
