Detailed COVID and IAD% Data

This table includes the key data that was used for plotting in this project. CDC reports aggregate counts of COVID-19 cases and death numbers daily online, based on the most recent numbers reported by states, territories, and other jurisdictions. They also, in partnership with the US Census Bureau, conduct the Household Pulse Survey which gathers information on the frequency of anxiety and depression symptoms.
